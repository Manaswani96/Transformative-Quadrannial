Career and Achievements of Dr. Rana el Kaliouby Dr. Rana el Kaliouby is a pioneer in affective computing, focusing on AI systems that understand and respond to human emotions. Key milestones in her career include: Affectiva: Co-founded Affectiva in 2009, a company that developed AI capable of reading emotions through facial expressions and voice tone. The technology enhances human-computer interaction and is used in industries like automotive, healthcare, and media. Technological Impact: Affectiva's Emotion AI helps brands and researchers analyze emotional responses to products and content. It’s applied in user engagement, customer service, and mental health diagnostics. Public Speaking & Advocacy: Dr. el Kaliouby advocates for ethical AI, focusing on privacy, bias, and equity. Her TED Talk, "This app knows how you feel", has garnered millions of views. Awards & Recognition: Recognized globally, with honors including Fast Company’s Most Creative People in Business (2017), MIT Technology Review’s 35 Innovators Under 35 (2018), and World Economic Forum’s Young Global Leader (2018). Books: Authored "This Is Your Brain on Tech", sharing her journey in AI and insights into the future of emotional technology.
