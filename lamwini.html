About Dr. Joy Buolamwini:

Education:
Dr. Joy Buolamwini is a highly accomplished computer scientist, widely known for her groundbreaking work on algorithmic bias, specifically in the context of facial recognition systems.
She completed her education across prestigious institutions:
  • Georgia Institute of Technology (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science)
  • Jesus College, Oxford (Master of Science in Social Science of the Internet)
  • Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) (Master of Science and PhD in Media Arts and Sciences)

Passion AND Motivation


Dr. Buolamwini is passionate about the intersection of technology and social justice, focusing on algorithmic bias in AI systems, especially in facial recognition.
Her motivation stems from personal experiences as a Black woman, where she faced underrepresentation in tech. This inspired her to create the Algorithmic Justice League, advocating for ethical, inclusive AI that benefits all and dismantles harmful biases against marginalized groups.

"The future of technology should be one where the systems we create reflect the diversity of the people who use them. Engineers must understand that the algorithms they build are not neutral, and they hold the power to shape the world in profound ways. The next generation of engineers has the opportunity—and the responsibility—to ensure that technology is designed to uplift, not marginalize."
- Dr. Joy Buolamwini
