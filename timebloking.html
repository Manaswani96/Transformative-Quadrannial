Hack: Allocate specific blocks of time for studying, 
    assignments, and breaks. Treat these as unbreakable appointments.
    How to Use?
    Use Google Calendar or a planner.
    Example:
    9 AM - 11 AM: Coding Assignment
    11 AM - 11:30 AM: Break
    11:30 AM - 1 PM: Math Practice
    Helps stay disciplined & minimizes distractions.
